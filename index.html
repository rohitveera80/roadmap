2. When to Start: A Semester-by-Semester Guide

Semesters 1-2 (First Year)

Join a coding club, master a foundational language (Python/C++), and build a strong grasp of Data Structures and Algorithms. Focus on problem-solving platforms like LeetCode.

Semesters 3-4 (Second Year)

Deep dive into domain-specific languages and libraries (e.g., Python for ML). Publish a mini-project on GitHub. Start learning SQL and core mathematical concepts for your domain.

Semesters 5-6 (Third Year)

Work on a significant, resume-worthy project. Start applying for summer internships. Gain proficiency in at least one cloud platform and explore advanced topics and frameworks.

Semesters 7-8 (Fourth Year)

Focus on a specialization. Contribute to open-source projects. Prepare for placements with mock interviews and advanced problem-solving. Finalize your capstone project.
